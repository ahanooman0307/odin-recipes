Fried Egg Recipe

If you want to have a delicious Fried Egg try this recipe.

This is a great go to when you have nothing else to eat.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • Dash of salt
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • OPTIONAL: 2 Wiri wiri pepper

Steps

  1. Bust the eggs into a bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper if you would like
  3. Beat the eggs up until smooth
  4. Add olive oil to fry pan and fry eggs on medium heat
  5. Turn off stove when eggs are fully fried

That's it. Enjoy!
