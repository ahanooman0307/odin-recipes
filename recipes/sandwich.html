Sandwich Recipe

If you want to have a delicious sandwich try this recipe.

This is very healthy and tasty

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 3 Slices of Chicken Breast
  • 1 tbsp of Mayonnaise
  • 3 Pieces of Lettuce

Steps

  1. Put the chicken and lettuce in the bread
  2. Add Mayonnaise on bread if you would like to

That's it. Enjoy!
