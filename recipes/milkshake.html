Milkshake Recipe

If you want to have a delicious milkshake try this recipe.

It is especially good after a nice workout

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Milk
  • 1/3 Cups of Oats
  • 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • OPTIONAL: 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients into blender
  2. Blend the ingredients

That's it. Enjoy!
